The PULA Philosophy:
A Bridge to a World Without Money

Beyond the humor and the crypto market frenzy, PULA of Dracula is an experiment with profound stakes: exploring a transition to a post-financial society. We use modern tools not to perpetuate the system, but to test its limits and imagine alternatives.

The Critique of the Symbol: Money vs. Wealth

Inspired by Alan Watts, we recognize modern man's fundamental error: we confuse the symbol (money) with reality (wealth). We chase numbers on a screen, forgetting true wealth: free time, community, ecosystem health. PULA, as a "payment unit," is a symbol ironically used to remind us of this confusion.

The MIND Principle: A New Measure of Success

Instead of measuring success by Market Cap alone, the PULAverse is guided by the MIND principle, proposed by Emad Mostaque: a balance between Material, Intelligence, Network, and Diversity. This is the true indicator of a thriving society.

🟢 Green P.U.L.A.: The Universal Perpetual Motion of Light and Water

The Green PULA initiative is our ecological manifesto. It is our vision to "make money grow on trees," redefining value in a sustainable way, in the spirit of Alan Watts' "leaving nature alone." We started with a simple experiment, the "Journal of the Immortal Pepper," to prove that real value is generated by the simplest forces of nature.
