The PULA Rules:
The Decalogue of the Wise Digital Rebel

Alright, hero. You got some PULA, you're feeling powerful, ready to conquer the world. But PULA, like any precious thing, comes with some unwritten rules... which we've written down here, to make it clear for everyone.   1. I. Thou shalt not covet thy neighbor's PULA more than thine own.

    To each their own PULA, as much as they could afford. Don't peek into your neighbor's wallet. Focus on yours and on how you can help the whole PULA community grow.

  2. II. Thou shalt not make for thyself a graven image of charts.

    The price of PULA, like any memecoin, can be unpredictable. Enjoy the ride, don't stress over every red or green candle.

  3. III. Remember the Pump.fun day and keep it holy (with responsible buys).

    Don't rush in like a ram at a new gate. Buy gradually to avoid "scaring" the price and to allow for organic growth.

  4. IV. Honor thy creator, the spirit of May Day, and Green PULA.

    Appreciate the "labor abolishment" vision behind the project. Support the community and contribute with ideas and content. PULA belongs to everyone.

  5. V. Thou shalt not dump thy PULA upon others.

    If you caught a good wave and your PULA has grown, be wise. Don't sell everything at once like a madman, you'll wreck the market for everybody. A true PULA holder knows how to take profits in moderation.

  6. VI. Thou shalt not be too PULA-greedy.

    Wanting a lot of PULA is natural. But greed corrupts. Diversify, don't put all your money into a single PULA, no matter how legendary it is.

  7. VII. Thou shalt not steal PULA memes (unless you share with credit).

    The community's creativity is the engine of PULA. Respect the work of others. Or better yet, create your own PULA masterpieces!

  8. VIII. Thou shalt not bear false witness against PULA (don't FUD).

    If you have questions or constructive criticism, express them civilly in the community. Don't spread panic or false information.

  9. IX. Thou shalt not covet another's PULA unless you are willing to buy it at a fair price.

    Don't expect handouts. PULA is earned through smart investment or by contributing to the community.

  10. X. Thou shalt DYOR (Do Your Own Research) before getting into PULA.

    Read the whitepaper, understand the project. Don't just listen to some guy at the pub telling you "PULA will make you a millionaire." Maybe it will, maybe it won't. At least know what you're getting into.
